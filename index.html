Buenos Aires MeetUp

April 27th, 2015
Darwin 1154, Buenos Aires, Argentina

The First Meetup

The idea is getting together to meet each other, figure out how much each of us knows about Clojure/ClojureScript and its ecosystem and what we are interested in.

Gal Dolber, one of our fellow clojurists, is going to give a talk about "Clojure-objc: native applications for iOS in Clojure" a library he is currently using as the CTO of a startup.

If you are interested in giving a talk or presenting a project please contact us and let us know.
